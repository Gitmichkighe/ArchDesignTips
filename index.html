ArchDesignTips

ArchDesignTips is an educational mobile application designed to support architecture students and design enthusiasts through concise, practical learning content.

About the App

The app provides 15,000+ short architectural tips organized into 80+ specialized categories. The content focuses on improving design thinking, technical understanding, and professional awareness in architecture.

Key Features

  • Over 15,000 carefully curated architectural tips
  • 80+ categories covering design, theory, construction, and practice
  • Short, easy-to-understand learning format
  • Designed specifically for architecture students

Audience

ArchDesignTips is intended for architecture students, interns, and anyone interested in architectural design and professional development.
